# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in TechBuddy respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Support accessibility-first and senior-friendly design decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly ignoring project standards after feedback.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

Report concerns to repository maintainers through a private GitHub issue or direct maintainer contact.

## Enforcement guidelines

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans from project spaces.
